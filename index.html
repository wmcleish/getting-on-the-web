THE BAND

We Are Emperor Jones



They used their Tufts degrees to start a band? Are they crazy? Yes, but sensical in having the where-with-all to choose the right path, not the road most traveled. Emperor Jones is a funky, rocky, bluesy band with a modernized feel. Coming from different parts of America and the world, their musical influence is vast, superimposing their musical composition. Currently gigging locally in the Boston area, Emperor Jones has their sights set on the rest of the globe, and they certainly contain the dynamic talent to do so.
